What's a Monte Carlo simulation?

Instead of solving probability with math formulas, what if you just... tried it a bunch of times? Flip a coin 10 times and you might get 7 heads. Flip it 10,000 times and you'll get almost exactly 50% heads. That's Monte Carlo: run random experiments until patterns emerge. Scientists use this to predict everything from stock markets to weather. Here, we're testing whether Jen's "lucky" imposter streak was actually lucky.
